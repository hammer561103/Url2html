Integration with DLP Systems

Combine the capabilities of TrueConf Enterprise with data leakage protection (DLP) systems that can block the transmission of confidential information based on configured filters.

Comprehensive Network Monitoring

Check the status of TrueConf Enterprise networks and infrastructure by combining the capabilities of a video conferencing solution with monitoring systems.

Two-factor Authentication
