TrueConf Coordinator

TrueConf Coordinator is a software and hardware system for managing meeting rooms and conference halls.

  • Control of cameras and microphones
  • Automation of typical scenarios for conference room use
  • Full compatibility with TrueConf and partners' equipment
  • Remote control and monitoring of equipment
  • Individual interface corresponding to the configuration of the room
Learn more

TrueConf Coordinator Operation Scheme

Flexible Automation Scenarios

Create scenarios for any event: adjust the order of automatic activation for speakerphones, screens, PTZ camera positions, and audio device volumes!

Interactive Hall Layout

Use the interactive layout of your meeting room or conference hall to manage equipment based on its actual location in the premises.

Personalization and Branding

Customize the interface's color scheme and add a logo that aligns with the company's corporate identity.

Work with Cameras

Managing and Selecting Presets

Adjust camera presets for quick control based on the event type.

Control of PTZ Cameras

Control the position and viewing angle of your PTZ cameras remotely in real time!

Audio and Video Matrix Management

Connect audio and video stream management systems to TrueConf Coordinator to instantly switch between input sources and output devices.

Flexible Sound Settings

Adjust the volume, turn on echo and noise reduction on all connected devices with just a few taps!

Equipment Monitoring

Remotely monitor equipment status and manage power supply.

Integration with TrueConf Group

Manage the connected TrueConf Group video conference endpoints in the meeting rooms.

Expand your capabilities with TrueConf Coordinator!
