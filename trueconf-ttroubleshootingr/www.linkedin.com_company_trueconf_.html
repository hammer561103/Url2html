About us

Founded in 2003, TrueConf is the largest vendor of enterprise and consumer products and equipment for video conferencing in Eastern Europe. TrueConf provides award-winning video conferencing software to connect desktops, mobiles, phones, H.323/SIP endpoints and much more across your entire organization. Tens of thousands of companies companies around the world choose TrueConf to meet their video conferencing needs. TrueConf native client apps are powered by 100% software architecture, and our products are available as both an on-premises solution and as a cloud service for end-customers and partners. http://trueconf.com +1 833 878-32-63
