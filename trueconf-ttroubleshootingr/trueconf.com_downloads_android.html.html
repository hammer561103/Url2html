Video Calls and Conferences on Android

Jump on video calls, hold group meetings, and collaborate with colleagues anytime, anywhere. Run your business effectively and stay on top of tasks with TrueConf for Android!

All-in-One App for Ultimate Business Communications

Discover cutting-edge video meeting and team messaging experience on your Android device!

Unlimited Meetings

Bring up to 1,500 participants to 4K video calls and enjoy engaging and productive collaboration with no time limits.

Content Sharing

Make presentations, share your smartphone screen, and boost your teamwork in real time.

Team Messaging

Communicate with your colleagues in personal and group chats both inside and outside of video conferences.

Control Your Meetings

Manage participants’ AV devices, assign presenters, remove users from the podium, and more.

Blurring and Replacing the Background

Participate in online meetings on the go or from makeshift workplaces without worrying about your background! Blur or replace it with a suitable image.

Real-time Interpretation

Participate in international conferences with real-time interpretation directly on your Android device!

Learn more

Dark Mode

TrueConf for Android supports dark mode with customizable color themes to match any late-night chatting styles and save battery life of your device.

Screen Annotations & Drawing

Take notes, highlight text phrases or draw over the content shared to focus your audience’s attention on what matters.

Professional Video Conferencing on Your Smartphone

All Participants in Sight

View all connected participants by swiping conference pages that display up to 9 users.

Speakers in Focus

Thanks to voice activity detection (VAD), active speakers are always displayed on the first conference page.

Quick Replies to Incoming Calls

Use quick answers or write a custom reply if you’re busy and cannot accept an incoming video call.

Reactions

Express your opinion and get swift feedback from your teammates right during a conference.

Conference Scheduling

Set the start time and date, schedule one-time or recurring meetings with co-workers, and set up custom video layouts for invited participants.

Not Just a Contact List

Navigate through your organization's departments and see instantly colleagues’ availability with presence statuses.

Compact Widget

If you need to reply to an incoming email or check the calendar, minimize your app and follow the discussion via the floating widget.

Focus on What Matters

TrueConf automatically generates video layouts so that only the most important is displayed on your smartphone screen.

Checking the equipment

Set up the camera, microphone, and headset before joining the conference.

Seamless Switching to Headphones

If you use headphones, TrueConf automatically connects them with an incoming video call, without any extra settings.

Always in Touch

Stay engaged and connected with teammates wherever you are, both inside and outside your corporate network, even if the app is not running.

Keep Communication from Any Device

Use your TrueConf account on multiple devices simultaneously and keep communication flowing — your calls and chat history is synced automatically.

Secure Platform

TrueConf Server is an advanced corporate communications platform deployed on your company's hardware, ensuring complete collaboration security. Exchange messages with colleagues in personal and group chats, and organize online meetings for up to 1,500 participants!
