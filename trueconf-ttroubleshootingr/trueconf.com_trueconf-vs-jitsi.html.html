TrueConf vs. Jitsi

TrueConf offers an enterprise-ready alternative to Jitsi web conferencing platform.

Looking for Jitsi alternatives?

Here are six reasons to consider TrueConf

Collaboration-first

Collaborate on shared files and documents with group chat, screen sharing, polling and recording.

Award-winning 4K video conferencing

Get your team together and run 4K video conferences with up to 1,500 participants.

Enterprise-ready

Turnkey enterprise-class solution embraced by tens of thousands of companies worldwide.

Easy-to-use

Installed and deployed within 15 minutes on a Windows Server in your companys network, doesnt require Linux admin skills.

Total interoperability

TrueConf natively works with the tools you like to enable one click to join from any platform or device.

SVC-based architecture

Low infrastructure requirements and stable operation on any device.

Protecting privacy without impacting quality

TrueConfs on-premises approach guarantees unrivaled privacy and communications control while all communications, meeting recordings and personal data stay within your company network under your supervision. On the other hand, as every open source software, Jitsi is vulnerable to multiple security risks due to wide availability of the source code.

Learn more

Client applications for any platform

TrueConf offers simplified team collaboration across any device thanks to cross-platform client apps. Additionally, every TrueConf app is integrated with unified communications features, such as address book, presence statuses, and telephony. As Jitsi provides in-browser meetings, its stability, performance and a set of features is limited by browser capabilities, which makes it an arguable choice for business use.

Download for free

One platform for all needs

With TrueConf, award-winning 4K video conferencing, collaboration tools and advanced interoperability are already integrated into a single product. To provide the same set of features, Jitsi requires the installation of additional software and custom development.

Learn more

Multi-vendor interoperability

TrueConf Server features native support for SIP and H.323 protocols and provides seamless integration with any video conferencing endpoints and telephony. In its turn, Jitsi only supports SIP and requires additional software installation to enable this option.

Virtual town hall meetings in 4K

With TrueConf, you can gather up to 1,500 participants in a 4K video meeting. Jitsi allows HD video conferences with up to 75 participants.

Simple guide to 4K video conferencing

Every IT administrators dream

IT administrators love TrueConf Server, because it can be installed instantly and deployed in 15 minutes in your companys network. To start using all Jitsi features, custom development and installation of additional software is required. With TrueConf, there is no need to additionally hire IT specialists or sign expensive contracts with system integrators. Just submit a ticket to our technical support and receive inclusive high-level assistance to get your video conferencing issues immediately solved.
