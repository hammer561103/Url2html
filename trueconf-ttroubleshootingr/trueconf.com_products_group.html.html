TrueConf Group

TrueConf Group is a hardware video conferencing endpoint for meeting rooms that supports SIP and H.323 protocols. It is equipped with a built-in MCU, and its modular configuration allows you to use any AV peripherals, providing the best video and sound quality.

Simplicity and Flexibility of Management

Full Compatibility with Video Conferencing Systems

Connect to any video conferencing endpoints, receive and send content, control the camera, and adjust the conference layout.

Compatibility with TrueConf Solutions

  • Automatic synchronization of contacts with TrueConf Server and TrueConf MCU
  • Cascading video conferences and calling TrueConf Server users.
  • Easy integration and management of hardware endpoints with TrueConf Coordinator

Conferences for Any Tasks

Host video meetings for any format of events in accordance with the tasks set!

Video call

Two participants see and hear each other.

All on screen

All participants can see and hear one another.

Smart meeting

Everyone can see and hear participants, when they start speaking.

Video lecture

The lecturer can see and hear all students while they can see and hear only the lecturer.

NDI Protocol Support

Stream video conferences to the local network and incorporate NDI-compatible devices into the endpoint layout. Use TrueConf Group for professional TV broadcasting and organization of live streams!

VNC Protocol support

Stream your computer or laptop screen to TrueConf Group without the need for a video capture card or a direct connection to an endpoint — it is sufficient to be on the same local network as the device.

Compensation for Network Packet Loss

Stay connected even at low internet speeds! TrueConf FEC algorithm compensates for 10% of lost network packets and ensures a stable connection among TrueConf Group, TrueConf Server, and TrueConf MCU.

Multi-level Security System

Support for H.235 and SRTP Encryption Protocols

Thanks to the support of the SRTP for SIP and H.235 for H.323 protocols, the endpoint not only ensures the secure transmission of multimedia data during a video conference, but also protects traffic using the AES encryption algorithm.

Ensuring Privacy at the Hardware Level

The PTZ cameras connected to the endpoint automatically turn away when switching to sleep mode or off, preventing the possibility of leakage of confidential information.

Offline Work in a Closed Network

TrueConf Group works autonomously inside your corporate network without an internet connection, guaranteeing complete confidentiality of your video communications.

Protection Against DDoS Attacks

The built-in technology for detecting DOS and DDoS attacks effectively neutralizes external cyber threats, ensuring stable and reliable video conferencing.

Blocking Spam Calls

The built-in smart protection mechanism against robotic and spam calls analyzes each subscriber, allowing only calls from genuine users and skipping those from robots or spammers.

Secure Network Connection

Support for IEEE 802.1X and IEEE 802. 1Q network standards ensure that the hardware endpoint only functions with verified devices within the local network.

Flexible Work with Content

Content Streaming

Send and receive any media data over the H.239 and BFCP protocols and transmit content via HDMI via the built-in capture card or wireless presentation system.

Showing Media Files

Share images, video files, presentations and documents with TrueConf Group! Set up an individual layout for displaying media files on a separate screen by controlling the show using an IR remote control or via the control panel of the endpoint.

Calendar of Upcoming Events

Receive invitations to video conferences in ICS format, and the events will be automatically added to your calendar!

User-friendly Management

Manage the endpoint, communication sessions, remote cameras of participants, and the conference layout using either the remote control or TrueConf Room Discovery application for Android devices.

Effective Use of the Meeting Room

Use TrueConf Group even when the device is in standby mode: connect a laptop and display the necessary information directly on the endpoint's screen!

Easy Integration with Meeting Room Management Systems

The ability to remotely control the endpoint using the command line via the SSH protocol enables the implementation of various interaction scenarios with equipment in meeting rooms.
