FAQ

What are meeting transcriptions?

Meeting transcriptions are written records of audio and video content that include all the details spoken during the meeting.

What are the benefits of using meeting transcription software?

Accuracy: the AI-based algorithm will capture even the smallest details of what you’ve said during your meeting, providing you with complete and readable records.

Saving time: You don’t have to pay note-takers to record important information or waste your time capturing meeting details.

Documenting meetings: you or your colleagues will always be able to catch up on what you missed and virtually attend all meetings.

How accurate is our transcription software?

Modern technological advances continue to improve the accuracy of automatic transcription.

TrueConf’s AI-based algorithm can:

•  allows you to capture the smallest details of your discussion;

•  recognises all of the mentioned terminology;

•  offers you an accurate transcription that is divided by speakers.
