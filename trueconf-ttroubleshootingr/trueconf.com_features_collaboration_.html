Collaboration Tools

Use the advanced capabilities of the video conferencing system to enhance teamwork productivity.

Team Messaging

Communicate in personal and group chats, and easily share images, presentations, documents, and files. Respond to colleagues at your convenience, while effortlessly transforming any discussion into a video meeting!

Learn more

Screen Sharing

Share the screen of your personal device and effortlessly showcase images, videos, and files to your colleagues in real time.

Drawing and Annotations

With the help of convenient drawing tools, easily place the necessary accents and add annotations.

Participants' Devices Management

The conference moderator can remotely control participants' devices — turn on or off users' cameras and microphones on the fly!

Learn more

Work with Content

File Sharing

Share images, videos, and documents with your colleagues, as well as view media files that are sent to the chat within the client application.

Viewing Video Files

Watch videos in TrueConf desktop client applications without installing additional software.

Desktop Remote Access

Transfer control of your desktop to colleagues to collaborate on a project or receive prompt assistance in troubleshooting technical problems.

Learn more

Smart Layouts

A layout with a priority window automatically prioritizes the speaking participant or the content being shared to the conference. Participate in meetings without getting distracted by setting up the layout!

Learn more

Virtual Background

Work from home, on the go, or at makeshift workplaces without having to worry about the space behind you! Use a background blur or completely replace it with a suitable image.

Learn more

Smart Noise Suppression

The smart noise suppression algorithm eliminates extraneous background noise and significantly improves the sound quality coming from users of TrueConf client applications, even when using budget headsets.

Learn more

Meeting Recording

Record conferences either in the desktop application or on the server to ensure important moments of the meeting are not missed and to easily share the recording with absent colleagues.

Learn more

Polls and Reactions

The opinions of meeting participants can be decisive — conduct polls, getting an instant response! Users can also utilize reactions to express their attitude towards what has been said.

Learn more

Real-Time Transcription

Engage in the discussion without getting distracted by taking notes! The built-in algorithm automatically converts the audio recording of the conference into a full text transcript, accurately aligning phrases with their respective timestamps.
