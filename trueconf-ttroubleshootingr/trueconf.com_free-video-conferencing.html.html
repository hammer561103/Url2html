Your Communications Stay Private

TrueConf provides the highest level of security via AES-256 encryption, which makes it a perfect solution for government organizations, state agencies and other companies that require full security and privacy. LAN prevents traffic interception and unauthorized access to your equipment. Your video, audio and content runs safely on your machine, and your administrator gets online monitoring and advanced reporting.

Scalable Video Coding Architecture

Thanks to support for Scalable Video Coding (SVC) each conference participant receives an individual set of video streams from other participants according to the conference layout, bandwidth, and the endpoint’s capabilities. The server itself does not perform transcoding, which significantly lowers infrastructure costs and requirements.

FAQ

How do I get started with TrueConf Server Free?

Download , install, and register TrueConf Server, create accounts for your employees in the User accounts section. Send login details to your employees and start video conferencing right away! Read our guide to learn how to do it in less than 15 minutes.

Is it safe to use TrueConf Server for video communication?

TrueConf video conferencing software provides the highest level of security via AES-256 encryption, GDPR compliance and ISO 27001-2013 certification, which makes it a perfect solution for government organizations, state agencies and other companies that require full security and privacy.

What is the maximum number of participants allowed in TrueConf video conferences?

TrueConf Server Free is suited for all video conferencing modes with up to 50 participants. With TrueConf Server full version, you can run meetings for up to 1500 participants.

How can I join TrueConf video conferences?

You can join TrueConf meetings from your client application, browser, or room endpoint. Learn more about how to join meetings in our article .

Can I take part in a video conference from a smartphone or tablet?

Yes, TrueConf offers native apps for iOS and Android. Alternatively, you can always join from your browser.
